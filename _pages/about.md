**I am a first-year PhD Student in the Department of Social and Political Sciences at [Bocconi University](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/Bocconi/SitoPubblico_EN/Navigation+Tree/Home/faculty+and+research/departments/Social+and+Political+Sciences/).** My research interests include political behavior, campaigns, and comparative political parties -- mainly in Western Europe and the US. Before joining Bocconi University, I earned master's degrees from Duke University (USA) and Erasmus University (NL) and participated in the Fulbright scholarship [program](https://foreign.fulbrightonline.org/).
