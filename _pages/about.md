Hi there! Welcome to my site. My name is Dirck de Kleer and I am a first-year PhD Student in Social and Political Science at Bocconi University. I am interested in political behavior, elections, and comparative political parties -- mainly in Western Europe and the US. Before joining Bocconi University, I earned master's degrees from Duke University (USA) and Erasmus University (NL) and was a Fulbright scholar.
