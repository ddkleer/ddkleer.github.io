I am a first-year PhD Student in the Department of Social and Political Sciences at [Bocconi University](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/Bocconi/SitoPubblico_EN/Navigation+Tree/Home/faculty+and+research/departments/Social+and+Political+Sciences/). My research interests include political behavior, campaigns, and comparative political parties -- mainly in Western Europe and the US. Before joining Bocconi University, I earned an MA in Liberal Studies (concentration: Political Science, History) from Duke University (USA) and a Graduate [Certificate](https://igs.duke.edu/academics/graduate-certificate-interdisciplinary-european-studies) in Interdisciplinary European Studies from Duke's Center for International And Global Studies. Prior to that, I also earned an MA in Media Studies (concentration: Political Communication) from Erasmus University (NL) and a Bachelor in Communication from Inholland University of Applied Sciences.
